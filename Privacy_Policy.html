Privacy Policy

Learn how Nawartona protects your data and ensures privacy for event invitations.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Effective Date: April 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Nawartona collects, uses, and protects the personal information provided to us for sending event invitations through our automated system using the WhatsApp Business API.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from event organizers:

  • Guest names
  • Guest phone numbers
  • Event date and location

2. How We Use This Information

The information is used solely to:

  • Send event invitations via WhatsApp
  • Receive attendance confirmations
  • Send event location and QR codes
  • Track attendance on the event day
  • Send thank-you messages after the event

3. Data Protection

All guest data is treated as confidential and used only for the purpose of managing the event. We do not store, share, or sell this data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

Guest data is automatically deleted within 1 day after the event concludes.
